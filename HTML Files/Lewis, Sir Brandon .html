1. Employment and earnings

From 27 January 2023 until further notice, Director of Woodlands Schools Ltd, 428 Rayleigh Road, Hutton, Brentwood CM13 1SD. I have the use of a company car valued at £10,000 per annum. I was due to receive an annual salary for this role but waived this on 1 February 2023. Hours: 8 hrs per month. I consulted ACoBA about this appointment. (Registered 21 February 2023; updated 27 February 2023, 01 March 2023 and 31 July 2023)
